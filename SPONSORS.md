# 💖 BijMantra Development Sponsorship Program

BijMantra is committed to becoming fully open source (Apache 2.0) in 2029. However, development requires sustainable funding. The Development Sponsorship Program allows institutions and enterprises to support open agricultural research while receiving **perpetual free licenses** and **public brand recognition**.

**Core Principle:** Those who can afford to contribute should do so, enabling free access for those who cannot. Sponsors invest in a global public good while receiving tangible benefits and long-term cost savings.

---

## 🏆 Sponsorship Tiers & Branding Benefits

Institutions and enterprises may choose to sponsor BijMantra development in lieu of annual licensing fees. Sponsors receive perpetual free licenses (Institutional or Commercial, based on organization type) plus public recognition and influence on development.

### TIER 1: SUSTAINING SPONSOR
**Contribution:** $10,000 - $25,000 USD per year (ongoing)

**Benefits:**
- Free institutional or commercial license (while actively sponsoring)
- **Small logo placement** on bijmantra.org sponsors page
- Named in the annual impact report
- Community recognition
- Standard support and updates

*Ideal for:* Institutions wanting to support open science with annual contributions while maintaining flexibility.

---

### TIER 2: DEVELOPMENT SPONSOR
**Contribution:** $50,000 USD one-time OR $25,000 USD/year for 3 years

**Benefits:**
- **Perpetual free license** (never pay licensing fees again; Commercial License granted to qualifying enterprises)
- **Medium logo placement** on `README.md` and bijmantra.org homepage
- Named in all software releases and version announcements
- Priority consideration for feature requests (not guaranteed, but weighted)
- Annual impact report showing how funds were used
- Public acknowledgment in presentations and publications
- Recognition as a Development Sponsor in perpetuity

*Ideal for:* Institutions wanting long-term cost savings plus prominent recognition for supporting open agricultural research.

---

### TIER 3: FOUNDING SPONSOR
**Contribution:** $100,000+ USD one-time OR $50,000 USD/year for 3 years

**Benefits:**
- All Development Sponsor benefits PLUS:
- **"Founding Sponsor" designation** (permanent recognition)
- **Large, prominent logo placement** on `README.md` and bijmantra.org homepage
- **Dedicated case study page** on bijmantra.org featuring your institution's story
- Quarterly meetings with the core development team (roadmap input)
- Co-branded case studies and research publications
- Speaking opportunities at BijMantra conferences and webinars
- First access to new features (beta testing program)
- Legacy recognition: "Built with support from [Institution]"

*Ideal for:* Institutions wanting to build a lasting legacy in open agricultural research while achieving significant long-term cost savings.

---

## 📈 Sponsorship vs. Licensing

Institutions may choose either standard licensing or development sponsorship:

**Comparison Example (High-Income Country, Large Institution):**

| Option | Year 1 | Year 5 | Year 10 | Total Cost |
|--------|--------|--------|---------|------------|
| Annual License | $25K | $125K | $250K | $250K |
| Development Sponsor | $50K | $50K | $50K | $50K |
| **Savings** | -$25K | $75K | $200K | **$200K** |

**Key Insight:** Development Sponsorship costs more in Year 1 but saves significantly over time while prominently highlighting your brand's commitment to open science.

## 🤝 How to Sponsor

### Step 1: Initial Inquiry
Contact: `sponsorship@bijmantra.org`  
Subject: "Development Sponsorship Inquiry - [Institution Name]"

Include:
- Institution name and location
- Current or anticipated BijMantra usage
- Preferred sponsorship tier
- High-resolution SVG/PNG logo files (light and dark variants preferred)

### Step 2: Consultation & Agreement
- We will arrange a call to discuss sponsorship benefits and structure.
- Review financial analysis and cost savings.
- Execute a customized sponsorship proposal and formal agreement.

### Step 3: Activation
- Processing of the contribution.
- Immediate activation of the perpetual license (Tier 2 and 3).
- **Logo and recognition placement on `README.md` and website.**
- Welcome announcement to the community.
