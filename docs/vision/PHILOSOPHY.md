## ЁЯОп Guiding Principles

### 1. Build Tools, Not Encyclopedias

> *"Does a breeder need this in their daily work?"*

Every feature must pass this test. We don't build comprehensive databases for their own sake тАФ we build tools that help make decisions.

**Anti-pattern:** A 10,000-field germplasm form that no one fills out.

**Our pattern:** Smart defaults, progressive disclosure, and the 20% of fields that matter 80% of the time.

---

### 2. Integrate, Don't Rebuild

> *"Stand on the shoulders of giants."*

The breeding community has built incredible resources: GRIN, Genesys, NCBI, BrAPI ecosystem. We connect to them, not compete with them.

**Anti-pattern:** Building our own sequence database.

**Our pattern:** Link to NCBI, display what breeders need, let specialists maintain the data.

---

### 3. Offline is Not an Edge Case

> *"The field is where breeding happens."*

For breeders in developing countries, connectivity is unreliable. Offline capability is not a nice-to-have тАФ it's essential.

**Anti-pattern:** "Requires internet connection."

**Our pattern:** PWA architecture with offline data collection and background sync. *(Core field workflows supported; full offline coverage is on the roadmap.)*

---

### 4. Free as in Freedom

> *"Knowledge belongs to humanity."*

Commercial software creates dependency. Open source creates capability. We choose capability.

**Anti-pattern:** Vendor lock-in, proprietary formats, subscription traps.

**Our pattern:** Open source, open standards, open data.

---

### 5. Complexity is Necessary, Confusion is Not

> *"Make things as simple as possible, but not simpler."* тАФ Einstein

Plant breeding is inherently complex. We don't hide that complexity тАФ we organize it so it becomes navigable.

**Anti-pattern:** Dumbing down to the point of uselessness.

**Our pattern:** Progressive disclosure, smart defaults, contextual help.

## ЁЯЪл What We Will Not Do

### We will not build terminator seeds

Our [Ethical Use Policy](../ETHICAL_USE_POLICY.md) explicitly prohibits use of Bijmantra for genetic use restriction technologies (GURTs) or any technology that restricts farmers' rights to save and replant seeds.

### We will not create vendor lock-in

Data entered into Bijmantra can always be exported. We use open standards. You can leave anytime.

### We will not paywall essential features

Core breeding functionality will always be free. Commercial licensing is for those who profit from selling software, not for those who use it.

### We will not compromise on interoperability

BrAPI compliance is not optional. If we can't exchange data with the ecosystem, we've failed.
