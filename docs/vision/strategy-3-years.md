## ðŸŽ“ Strategic Principles

### 1. User-Centric Development
**Every feature must solve a real breeder problem**
- Talk to 10 users before building
- Validate with prototypes
- Measure impact after launch

### 2. Open Core Model
**Core platform remains open source**
- Community-driven development
- Enterprise features are paid
- No lock-in, data portability

### 3. Integration Over Invention
**Connect to existing systems, don't rebuild**
- BrAPI for breeding data
- NCBI for genomics
- ERPNext for ERP
- Google Earth Engine for GIS

### 4. Mobile-First, Offline-First
**Works in the field, not just the office**
- Responsive design
- Offline capability
- Low bandwidth mode
- Touch-friendly UI

### 5. AI-Augmented, Not AI-Replaced
**AI assists breeders, doesn't replace them**
- Explainable AI
- Human-in-the-loop
- Confidence scores
- Override capability
