# Sponsor BijMantra

Last reviewed: 2026-02-25
Owner: BijMantra Maintainers

## Why Sponsor

BijMantra builds open agricultural intelligence infrastructure that connects fragmented scientific domains into practical, explainable decision systems.

Your sponsorship supports:

- open research infrastructure,
- interoperable APIs and standards,
- reproducible and transparent decision workflows,
- faster innovation for agricultural resilience.

- GitHub Sponsors: `https://github.com/sponsors/denishdholaria`
- Open Collective: `https://opencollective.com/bijmantra`

## Where Funds Are Used

1. Core platform engineering and maintenance.
2. Testing, reliability, and CI hardening.
3. Public documentation and onboarding quality.
4. Cross-domain module development.
5. Community support and contributor enablement.

## Sponsor Profiles We Welcome

- Individuals supporting open science software.
- Research labs and universities.
- Seed, agritech, and agricultural data companies.
- Foundations and public-interest organizations.

## Engagement Options

1. General sponsorship to support core development.
2. Program sponsorship for a domain module.
3. Institutional partnership for co-development.

## Transparency and Accountability

- Public roadmap and release notes where appropriate.
- Open repository and visible development activity.
- Clear scope and milestone definition for funded work.
