# Crop Climate Resilience

This module deals with planetary-scale existential risk. 
The immediate challenge is that agricultural data points are historically local. A breeder tests a seed varietal under *current* local conditions.

## The Problem
What happens when the local micro-climate shifts by 2°C over the next decade?

## The Architecture Specification
BijMantra requires a dedicated engine to ingest macroscopic climate prediction models (e.g., CMIP6 outputs) and mathematically map them against the localized phenotypic expressions recorded via the [Genetics Engine](genetics-engine).

We are seeking **Atmospheric Scientists** and **Climatologists** to architect the data pipelines required to compute these probabilities locally on a resource-constrained offline device.
