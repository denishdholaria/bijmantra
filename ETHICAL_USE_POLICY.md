## PREAMBLE

BijMantra is built on the principle that agricultural technology must support:

* farmer sovereignty
* food system resilience
* long-term ecological sustainability

This Policy ensures the Software is not used in ways that undermine these principles.

---

## 1. ABSOLUTE PROHIBITIONS

The following uses are strictly prohibited under all circumstances:

### 1.1 Genetic Use Restriction Technologies (GURTs)

The Software may NOT be used in connection with:

* Terminator seed technologies (V-GURTs)
* Seed dependency systems (T-GURTs)
* Chemical activation systems designed for commercial lock-in
* Any mechanism that prevents seed saving or reuse

**Burden of Proof:** The user must demonstrate absence of exploitative dependency.

---

### 1.2 Biological Weapons and Harmful Applications

The Software may NOT be used for:

* Biological weapon development
* Creation or enhancement of harmful pathogens
* Agricultural sabotage

---

### 1.3 Deceptive, Fraudulent, or Misleading Practices

The Software may NOT be used to:

* Falsify data or research outputs
* Misrepresent agricultural products
* Commit fraud in certification or compliance systems
* Deceive farmers, institutions, or stakeholders

Additionally:

* **Impersonation of BijMantra is explicitly prohibited**
* **Deployment of misleading or lookalike systems is prohibited**
